# Bug Report: Review Text Changes to All Caps

## Summary
Newly submitted product reviews are displayed in all uppercase, even when the customer enters normal sentence casing.

## Severity
Medium

## Current Behavior
Review text appears in all caps after submission.

## Expected Behavior
Review text should appear exactly as entered by the customer.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the shop and go to any product detail page.
2. In the Customer Reviews section, enter a name, any star rating and a review using mixed-case text (example: "This Should Stay Mixed Case").
3. Submit the review.
4. Look at the newly posted review in the list.

## Impact
- Customer-written content is altered unexpectedly.
- Reviews look less natural and less trustworthy.
- Overall content quality feels lower on product pages.

## Frequency
Consistent for newly submitted reviews.
